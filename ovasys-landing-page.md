## DESIGN NOTES

- **Layout:** Left-aligned text. Heavy whitespace. Subtle grid.
- **Color:** Monochrome base + one sharp accent color (your call — electric blue, warm amber, or pure white on black).
- **Typography:** Technical, confident. Avoid generic fonts (no Inter, no Roboto). Consider: Syne, Neue Haas Grotesk, Monument Grotesk, or a serif contrast pairing.
- **Motion:** Subtle. Fade-ins on scroll. No gimmicks.
- **Feel:** Infrastructure, not portfolio. System, not studio.
- **References:** Linear.app, Vercel.com, early Stripe.com
